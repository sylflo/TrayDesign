TRAY DESIGN

Why do we need a design language?

Designing for different platforms,
stay aligned

As the e-commerce business develops, people access information from an increasing number of devices, it's important to ensure that design is consistent and optimized to meet our business objectives and create enjoyable experience for users.

The most effective way to ensure that the team (both Online and Offline) is on the same page when designing separate parts of the website or for different devices, is to create a design style guide.

Overview

We chose to use layers help to differentiate between the different kinds of tasks performed by the components, making it easier to create a design that supports reusability of components.

Tray Design is comprised of three layers, Desktop Layer, Tray Layer and Gadget (content) layer, each layer performing a specific type of task.
